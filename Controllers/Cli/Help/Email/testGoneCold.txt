testGoneCold
----------------
Sends the GoneCold email to a specific user.

Required parameters:
--guid=user_guid


Optional parameters:
--send=* actually send the email
--output=path/to/output.html save the generated email body to the disk.

Running it in docker:
docker exec -it minds_php-fpm_1 php /var/www/Minds/engine/cli.php email testGoneCold --guid=959472937935573007 --output=/var/www/Minds/engine/gone_cold.html  --send
